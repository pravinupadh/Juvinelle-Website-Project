About Us

Juvenile Eduventures

Juvenile Eduventures Pvt. Ltd.

JUVENILE EDUVENTURES (JED) believe travel plays an integral part in education. Any excursion out of the classroom is remembered by students as among the highlights of their education Not only is it a break from the norm, but it also provides a profound and unique learning opportunity: travel can develop social and team-building skills, stimulate personal growth, bring new perspective about the world and its many cultures and customs, and ultimately revitalize interest and passion in the very activity of learning. Travel can broaden horizons.
Organizing an educational school trip can prove a challenging endeavour, but our student tour specialists take care of all the details from initial contact through to tour feedback and review on your return home, allowing teachers to focus on educating, with minimal additional homework to do. You will be designated an JUVENILE EDUVENTURES specialist, who will be on-hand to assist throughout the entire process: from creating a customized itinerary to suit your educational travel requirements, pre-trip consultation and logistical organization, through to full trip support, and post-tour follow-up. For even greater peace of mind, one of our team members can also accompany your group on tour.
Our well-travelled JUVENILE EDUVENTURES team has a huge breadth of destination expertise and decades of experience planning student group tours of primary importance to our operation and philosophy is the safety and comfort of your group – as such, we only partner with reliable service providers with proven safety records who adhere to industry-approved safety standards.
